AR Rahuman Songs

A.R. Rahman, a renowned composer and musician, has created many iconic and influential songs across a variety of films. Here are some of his most notable tracks:

1) "Mazhai Kuruvi"

  • Movie: Chekka Chivantha Vaanam (2018)
  • Singers: A.R. Rahman
  • Composer: A.R. Rahman
  • Lyricist: Vairamuthu
  • Impact: The song is well-regarded for its serene and melodic nature, showcasing A.R. Rahman’s versatility as both a composer and a vocalist. "Mazhai Kuruvi" stands out in the Chekka Chivantha Vaanam soundtrack for its ability to evoke emotions through its intricate musical arrangement and heartfelt lyrics. The track’s soothing melody and lyrical beauty have made it a favorite among fans of A.R. Rahman and Tamil film music.

2) "Azhagiye"

  • Movie: Kaatru Veliyidai (2017)
  • Singers: Arjun Chandy, Haricharan, Jonita Gandhi
  • Composer: A.R. Rahman
  • Lyricist: Madhan Karky
  • Impact: The song quickly became popular for its upbeat tempo and modern vibe, resonating with listeners who appreciate a blend of romance and energy in music. "Azhagiye" stands out in the Kaatru Veliyidai soundtrack as a feel-good anthem that celebrates the joyous side of love. Its catchy tune and engaging lyrics have made it a favorite among fans of A.R. Rahman, contributing to the film’s musical success.

3) "Nenjukkule"

  • Movie: Kadal (2013)
  • Singers: Shakthisree Gopalan
  • Composer: A.R. Rahman
  • Lyricist: Vairamuthu
  • Impact: "Nenjukkule" was widely praised for its simplicity and emotional depth, becoming one of the standout tracks from the Kadal soundtrack. The song’s soulful melody and heartfelt lyrics struck a chord with listeners, making it a favorite among fans of A.R. Rahman and Tamil music lovers. Its ability to evoke deep emotions through its understated beauty has made "Nenjukkule" a memorable and cherished song in Tamil cinema.

4) "Nenjae yezhu"

  • Movie: Maryan (2013)
  • Singers: A.R. Rahman
  • Composer: A.R. Rahman
  • Lyricist: Kutti Revathi
  • Impact: The song has been widely appreciated for its powerful message and emotional depth. "Nenje Ezhu" stands out in the Maryan soundtrack as an anthem of hope and perseverance, touching the hearts of listeners with its evocative melody and encouraging lyrics. The track’s ability to inspire and uplift has made it a favorite among fans of A.R. Rahman and those seeking motivation through music. Its stirring composition and heartfelt performance have solidified "Nenje Ezhu" as one of the most memorable songs in Tamil cinema.

5) "Mannipaaya"

  • Movie: Vinnaithaandi Varuvaayaa (2010)
  • Singers: A.R. Rahman, Shreya Ghoshal
  • Composer: A.R. Rahman
  • Lyricist: Thamarai
  • Impact: The song is widely regarded as one of the most beautiful and touching tracks in the Vinnaithaandi Varuvaayaa soundtrack. "Mannipaaya" resonates deeply with listeners due to its emotional intensity and relatable themes of love and remorse. The combination of Rahman’s moving composition and Shreya Ghoshal’s soulful rendition has made it a favorite among fans of Tamil music. The track’s ability to evoke deep emotions and its lyrical beauty have solidified its place as one of the most memorable songs in Tamil cinema.
