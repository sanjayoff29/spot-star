STR Songs

Simbu (STR), also known as Silambarasan, is a popular Tamil actor, singer, and lyricist. He has delivered several hit songs in Tamil cinema. Here’s a list of some of his most memorable songs where he served as the singer (and sometimes as the lyricist):

1) "Beep Song"

  • Independent Single
  • Composer: STR, Anirudh Ravichander
  • Lyricist: STR
  • Impact: The "Beep Song" severely impacted Simbu's reputation, leading to widespread criticism, legal actions, and public backlash for its offensive lyrics. It sparked debates on artistic responsibility and free speech, ultimately damaging Simbu's public image, though he later apologized and continued his career in the industry.

2) "Evan Di Unna Pethan"

  • Movie: Vaanam (2011)
  • Composer: Yuvan Shankar Raja
  • Lyricist: STR
  • Imapct :"Evan Di Unna Pethan," from the movie Vaanam (2011), had a significant impact due to its bold lyrics and catchy tune. Written and sung by Simbu (STR), the song became a massive hit, resonating with the youth for its rebellious and straightforward expression of unrequited love. However, it also faced criticism for its provocative lyrics, which some felt were too direct. Despite this, the song cemented Simbu's reputation as a bold and unconventional artist, contributing to the film's popularity and becoming an anthem for those experiencing love and frustration.
