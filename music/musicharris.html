Harris Jayaraj Songs

Harris Jayaraj is a well-known Indian music composer who has composed many memorable songs in Tamil cinema. His music is known for its melodious tunes, innovative arrangements, and catchy beats. Here are some notable songs composed by Harris Jayaraj:

1) "Thee Illai "

  • Movie: Engeyum Kadhal (2011)
  • Singers: Naresh Iyer, Mahathi
  • Composer: Harris Jayaraj
  • Lyricist: Thamarai
  • Impact: The song was well-received for its dynamic energy and catchy tune, making it a popular choice among fans of upbeat Tamil music. "Thee Illai" contributed to the success of the Engeyum Kadhal soundtrack, showcasing Harris Jayaraj’s ability to blend modern sounds with traditional Tamil music elements. The vibrant feel of the song made it a favorite for dance numbers and casual listening alike.

2) "Adiyae Kolluthey "

  • Movie: Vaaranam Aayiram (2008)
  • Singers: Krish, Benny Dayal, Shruti Haasan
  • Composer: Harris Jayaraj
  • Lyricist: Thamarai
  • Impact: The song became an instant hit for its energetic vibe and unique sound, different from the usual romantic ballads. "Adiye Kolluthey" was particularly popular among the youth and became a signature song of Vaaranam Aayiram. It showcased Harris Jayaraj’s versatility as a composer, capable of creating diverse and modern music. The song remains one of the most memorable tracks in Tamil cinema and continues to be celebrated for its innovative composition and powerful delivery.

3) "Annul Maelae"

  • Movie: Vaaranam Aayiram (2008)
  • Singers: Sudha Ragunathan
  • Composer: Harris Jayaraj
  • Lyricist: Thamarai
  • Impact: The song is celebrated for its classical touch and the emotional depth it brings to the narrative of Vaaranam Aayiram. "Annal Mele Panithuli" received widespread acclaim for its unique blend of classical and contemporary music, showcasing Harris Jayaraj’s ability to innovate while respecting traditional musical forms. Sudha Ragunathan’s vocals added a layer of authenticity and grace to the song, making it a favorite among fans of both classical and film music. The song’s timeless quality and lyrical beauty have helped it remain a beloved track in Tamil cinema.

4) "Ava Enna"

  • Movie: Vaaranam Aayiram (2008)
  • Singers: Karthik, V.V. Prasanna
  • Composer: Harris Jayaraj
  • Lyricist: Thamarai
  • Impact: The song became one of the most popular tracks from Vaaranam Aayiram, resonating with audiences for its heartfelt lyrics and Karthik’s emotive singing. "Ava Enna" is often praised for its ability to convey deep emotions, making it a go-to song for those going through similar experiences of love and loss. The combination of Harris Jayaraj’s soulful composition and Thamarai’s touching lyrics has made "Ava Enna" a timeless favorite in Tamil music. The song's enduring popularity is a testament to its powerful emotional appeal.

5) "Nenjukkul Peidhidum"

  • Movie: Vaaranam Aayiram (2008)
  • Singers: Hariharan, Devan Ekambaram, V.P. Prasanna
  • Composer: Harris Jayaraj
  • Impact: "Nenjukkul Peidhidum" became an instant classic upon its release and remains one of the most beloved tracks in Tamil cinema. The song is lauded for its lyrical beauty, musical sophistication, and Hariharan’s soulful rendition, which captures the subtleties of love in a profound way. The picturesque imagery created by Thamarai’s lyrics, combined with Harris Jayaraj’s mesmerizing melody, has made "Nenjukkul Peidhidum" a perennial favorite among fans. Its timeless appeal lies in its ability to evoke deep emotions and transport listeners into a world of romance and longing.
