Anjaan

"Anjaan" (2014) is a Tamil-language action film directed by Lingusamy and stars Suriya in the lead role. The film also features Samantha Ruth Prabhu, Vidyut Jammwal, and Soori in key roles.

The story revolves around Raju (played by Suriya), a fearless and skilled gangster who operates in Mumbai. The film begins with Raju's return to Chennai after a long time, and he is determined to seek revenge for his brother's death. Raju's brother, Ravi (played by Suriya in a dual role), was a well-known gangster who was killed by a rival gang led by Vidyut Jammwal's character, Ravi's former associate.

Raju’s quest for vengeance leads him into a series of violent confrontations with Mumbai's underworld. As he delves deeper into the criminal world, he uncovers shocking truths about his brother's death and the extent of the conspiracy against him.

The film combines high-octane action sequences with elements of drama and suspense. Raju, with his unwavering determination and combat skills, takes on powerful enemies and uncovers hidden truths about his brother's past. Alongside the action, the film also explores themes of brotherhood, loyalty, and the cost of revenge.

"Anjaan" is noted for its stylish presentation, intense action, and Suriya’s powerful performance in dual roles. The movie features a mix of thrilling action, emotional moments, and dramatic plot twists.
